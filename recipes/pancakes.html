Homemade Pancakes

Easy, classic, homemade Pancakes! These are fluffy, tender, buttery, golden brown, and lightly sweet â€“ everything a pancake should be. Way better than the packaged mix and made with everyday staple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups (189g) all-purpose flour (preferably unbleached), scoop and level to measure
  • 2 Tbsp (25g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/3 cups whole milk
  • 3 Tbsp butter, melted (unsalted or salted is fine)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp plain vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
